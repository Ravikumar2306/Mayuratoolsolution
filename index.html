ELECTRO PLATED WHEELS

The electroplated diamond grinding wheel is made by the electrochemical method.
The working layer of the grinding wheel contains diamond abrasives which are bonded to the matrix by the metal bond. Firstly, deposit the metal bond and the thickness is 20% of the diamond abrasive height, and then the diamond abrasive is further bonded with the metal bond, and the thickness of the deposited metal bond is about 2/3 of the diamond abrasive height. It can be made of tools of various complex shapes with high precision.
For rotary diamond dresser with high precision, electroplating is ideal choice.
The coating is well distributed on the matrix, uniform in thickness and not limited by the shape of the matrix.
Electroplating technology is simple, easy to mold, can dress short manufacturing cycle.

DIAMOND ROLLER DRESSER AND LAPPING PROCUCTS

The diamond roller dresser (rotary dresser)is a kind of diamond roller tools for dressing grinding wheel that a working surface with much small grain diamond. The working principle is that conventional grinding wheels or CBN grinding wheels are forming dressed by the diamond roller dresser installed on the grinding machine, and after dressing the grinding wheel and then grind the parts, so that the contour, precision, size of the diamond roller dresser are copied through the grinding wheel to the surface of the parts to be ground.
